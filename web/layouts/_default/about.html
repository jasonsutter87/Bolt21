Our Mission

Bolt21 was built with a simple goal: make self-custodial Lightning payments accessible to everyone. No technical knowledge required. No compromises on security.

We're particularly focused on helping small-scale Bitcoin miners receive their payouts without losing a significant portion to on-chain fees. With BOLT12 support, miners using Ocean can receive daily payouts of any size directly to their Lightning wallet.

What We Believe

Self-Custody First

Your keys, your Bitcoin. Always. We will never hold your funds or have access to your recovery phrase.

Simplicity

Bitcoin is complex enough. Your wallet shouldn't be. We strip away the unnecessary to focus on what matters.

Transparency

Open source code means you can verify everything we do. Trust, but verify.

Community

Built by Bitcoiners, for Bitcoiners. We're part of the community we serve.

100% Open Source

Every line of code is public and auditable. We believe security comes from transparency, not obscurity.
