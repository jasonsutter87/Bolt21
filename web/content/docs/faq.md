# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is Bolt21?
Bolt21 is a self-custodial Bitcoin Lightning wallet with BOLT12 support. It's designed for simplicity and security, perfect for receiving mining payouts or everyday Lightning payments.

### Is Bolt21 free?
Yes! Bolt21 is completely free to download and use. You only pay standard Lightning Network fees for transactions.

### Is my Bitcoin safe?
Your Bitcoin is as safe as your recovery phrase. Bolt21 is self-custodial, meaning only you control your funds. We never have access to your Bitcoin.

## Wallet Security

### What is a recovery phrase?
Your 12-word recovery phrase is the master key to your wallet. Anyone with these words can access your funds. Store them safely offline - never share them with anyone.

### What if I lose my phone?
No problem! Download Bolt21 on a new device and restore using your 12-word recovery phrase. Your funds will be fully recovered.

### Can I use Face ID / Touch ID?
Yes! Bolt21 supports biometric authentication for quick and secure access to your wallet.

## Payments

### How do I receive Bitcoin?
Tap Receive and share your BOLT12 offer or on-chain address. For recurring payments like mining payouts, use your BOLT12 offer.

### How do I send Bitcoin?
Tap Send, paste a Lightning invoice or BOLT12 offer, enter the amount (if needed), and confirm the payment.

### What's the minimum payment amount?
Lightning payments can be as small as 1 satoshi (0.00000001 BTC). Practical minimums depend on network fees.

## Ocean Mining

### How do I receive Ocean payouts?
1. Copy your BOLT12 offer from Bolt21
2. Paste it in your Ocean pool dashboard as the payout address
3. Payouts arrive automatically - daily or as you earn

### What's the minimum payout?
Ocean can pay out as little as 2,000 sats via Lightning - much lower than on-chain minimums.

### Why use Lightning for mining payouts?
- No on-chain fees eating your profits
- Instant settlement
- Daily payouts possible
- Works great for small miners
