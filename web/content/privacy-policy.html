Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

Bolt21 is a self-custodial Bitcoin Lightning wallet. We are committed to protecting your privacy and believe that financial sovereignty includes data sovereignty. This policy explains what information we collect (spoiler: almost nothing) and how we handle it.

Self-Custodial Means Self-Sovereign

Bolt21 is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Your keys stay on your device. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrase.
  • No account required. You don't need to provide an email, phone number, or any personal information to use Bolt21.
  • No KYC. We don't collect identity documents or perform identity verification.
  • Local-first. Your wallet data is stored locally on your device.

What We Don't Collect

Unlike traditional financial apps, Bolt21 does NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Your location or IP address
  • Your transaction history
  • Your Bitcoin balance
  • Your contacts or address book
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

Lightning Network Considerations

Bolt21 uses the Breez SDK to connect to the Lightning Network. When you make Lightning payments:

  • Payment routing information is processed by Lightning Service Providers (LSPs) to facilitate payments
  • This is inherent to how the Lightning Network operates
  • We recommend reviewing Breez's privacy policy for details on their data handling

BOLT12 and Ocean Mining

When using BOLT12 offers for Ocean mining payouts:

  • Your BOLT12 offer is shared with Ocean to enable payouts
  • Ocean's privacy policy governs how they handle this information
  • Bolt21 does not intermediate or store this data
